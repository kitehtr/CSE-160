✦ Wow Point Features

  • Animated Starfield: 6,000 BufferGeometry particles each with a randomized drift speed, wrapping infinitely.
  • Animated Engine Exhaust Flames: Flickering emissive cone meshes
  • UnrealBloomPass: Post-processing HDR bloom via EffectComposer, making all emissive objects glow.
